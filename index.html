FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

Common questions from Dubai real estate agencies before going live with AI.

Setup typically takes 48 hours from the time we receive your agency details. This includes AI voice configuration, WhatsApp Business API connection, CRM integration, and a live test call with your team. We handle everything — no technical knowledge required on your end.
The AI uses a professional, warm female voice that sounds natural and conversational. Most callers do not realise they are speaking with an AI. The AI introduces itself as your property assistant, which is fully compliant and builds trust with buyers who appreciate instant, 24/7 responses.
We integrate directly with Bayut, PropertyFinder, and Dubizzle lead feeds. For CRM, we support Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Pipedrive, and most Dubai-based agency systems via API or Zapier. Custom integrations are available on the Growth and Elite plans.
The AI is trained to handle property inquiries, qualification, and scheduling. If a buyer asks something outside its scope — such as highly specific legal or mortgage questions — it politely informs the buyer and offers to have a human agent follow up. The lead and query are automatically flagged in your CRM for agent review.
Yes. The AI supports Arabic, English, Russian, French, and Mandarin — the five most common buyer languages in Dubai. Language detection is automatic. You can also set a preferred primary language per listing or number. Multi-language support is available on Growth and Elite plans.
All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contract. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time with 30 days' notice. The one-time setup fee is non-refundable as it covers the configuration and onboarding work completed before go-live.
The moment a lead comes in, the AI sends a personalised WhatsApp message AND places a voice call simultaneously — within 3 seconds. Whichever channel the buyer responds on first, the AI continues the conversation there. All interactions sync to your CRM in real time so your agents always have full context.
